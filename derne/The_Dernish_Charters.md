

**The Dernish Charters**

*The documents enclosed within are set to govern the city of Derne and its local waters and land territory. All superior laws and rules are enforced in accordance with charter addenda.*

# **Preamble**

	In accordance with superior law ascribed in the addenda of these charters, the City of Derne is governed via the enclosed documents of its charters. Dernish guests and citizens are to follow all laws included and above.

# **I \- The Council of Chairs**

1. The Council of Chairs have total authority over the city of Derne. Their leader, the Mayor, holds the city’s official diplomatic power.  
2. The Council of Chairs creates and enforces policy through simple majority. Any player may appeal to the Council of Chairs to make a resolution on a topic, and any one Chair may table and discuss it, at their discretion.  
3. The Council of Chairs can elevate any proposal from an internal majority vote to a majority poll of the populace.  
4. The Council of Chairs is beholden to the Citizens and Residents of Derne- if suspected inactive, treasonous, or incompetent, the Citizens and Residents of Derne may field a petition in discord.  
5. **See the First Amendment.**  
6. All lawmaking, regulatory, and punitive actions taken by the Council of Chairs must be publicly posted for public and superior law review.  
7. In the event of any abdication or deposition of a Chair, re-elections are to be held immediately, and to be terminated within a week, as a single transferable vote election. If the abdication or deposition is during an active Council of Chairs election, the other Council members may act in their stead until the election is over.

**City Guide Exception**  
	Any citizen or resident of Derne may be given the title of City Guide by Council of Chairs majority. They have access to all public Derne namelayers and are expected to give access to newfriends. City Guides also can give plots to players in accordance with the wishes of the Council of Chairs. In addition to those requirements, City Guides must report any new player to the Council of Chairs after touring is completed. City Guides are to be given invite access to all public Derne namelayers.

# **II \- Land Ownership and Development**

1. Citizens have the right to own a plot determined by any Chair within the borders of Derne. Once claimed, they must submit or discuss a formal building plan with a Chair to maintain dimensional and aesthetic compliance with other plots.  
2. Non-citizens and non-residents may own plots, also determined by Chair. These plots must have build plans with pictures submitted to the Council of Chairs before significant building has gone underway.  
3. Any plot owner reserves the right to build storage, temporary animal accommodations, and temporary shelter on a plot until their build plan is approved.  
4. A Chair may derelict a building for any reason. If they do so, the owner has 1 week to take the dispute to the Icarian council or else ownership of the plotted land returns to the City of Derne. Citizens may derelict buildings similarly by posting in the discord channel for it.  
5. Derelictions and build plans proposed by Dernites are handled via the discord, following the templates provided there.  
6. The Council of Chairs reserves the rights to control any unclaimed plot as a “Development Project.” Those plots cannot be claimed by citizens without a direct appeal to the Council of Chairs.  
7. The Council of Chairs may deem a building or parcel a historical site or religious site. Those locations can only be derelicted with the approval of the entire Council.

# **III \- Census, Citizenship, and Voting**

1. A citizen’s voting rights may be stripped for an election cycle if they are non-religiously pearled for more than 24 hours, banned from the CivMC server itself, at the discretion of the Council of Chairs.  
2. Any Dernish Citizen may vote in public polls and referenda, and each of their votes is equal.  
3. For the election of the Council of Chairs, only Citizens and Dual citizens may vote.  
4. A Dernish Citizen is someone with the role on discord, which gets purged 3 days after each election is held.  
5. The Council of Chairs must approve citizenship requests.  
6. Dernish Citizens reserve the right to field a petition to the Derne government. The Mayor may table it for immediate discussion, or if it reaches 50% active population vote within seven days, it must be tabled for discussion. Petitions for the removal of a Derne government official must go to superior government law.

# **IV \- Delegation and Authorization**

1. The Council of Chairs may delegate tasks, committees, and roles as necessary.  
2. The Council of Chairs appoints the leader for committees. Any Derne citizen may be recruited by them.  
3. Any formal trade agreements, laws, warrants, or Letters of Marque from the Council of Chairs must be notarized by a legal notary qualified by superior law, unless in a state of collapse.

# **Provision of Collapse**

1. In the event of a failed election, no government staff available for an extended period, or a mass-grief of Derne, State of Collapse vote amongst residents may be brought forward.  
2. Should superior be impotent or also collapsed, a Guardian or someone with name layer access may be utilized to re-establish permissions and allocation of resources for the City of Derne to a Citizen. After they execute the transfer, they must recuse themselves from the discord roles and namelayer roles and be struck from the Trusted Non-Native list permanently.  
3. A State of Collapse is concluded when the provisional government deems it to be over or a public referendum is passed with a 50% vote.

# **Provision of Vacation and Absences**

1. Any Dernish titleholder may put to a public poll or referenda a temporary replacement while they take a vacation. If their break is longer than a month, they should abdicate their title and transfer ownership of their namelayers to another Dernish Citizen.  
2. Dernish non-active players may participate in elections if given waiver by the Council of Chairs for the same reason. Additionally, Dernish Citizens are advised to have at least one other person as admin on all of their namelayers for security and vacation purposes.

# **Addenda 1 - Elections**

	Chair elections are held for three seats, in a **Single Transferable Vote**. After the Chairs are elected, the chairs themselves appoint the Mayor of Derne, from the Council of Chairs. In the event of a tie, it is broken by runoff voting. Elections are to be held once per month, starting from the day of ratification of the Charters.

# **Addenda 2 - Superior Law**

	Icenia is the Superior Law to Derne, and the regional governments it prescribes over Derne as well.

# **Amendment 1 - the Keyholder’s Bill**

Stewardship of the Discord, Namelayers, and other systems that persist between councils are to be managed by Keyholders. A Keyholder is a trusted party who will manage the listed at the behest of the democratically elected government and in accordance with the Dernish constitution. A Keyholder can only be added or removed via a majority vote of the populace, which may be fielded by any citizen. The Council may choose to veto a keyholder being added.

Raeders, Haxxor, Zolo, and Dr. Bacon Hair are to be Keyholders.
