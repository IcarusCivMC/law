# The Treaty of Myra, initialized Dec. 4 2023

 *Conflict shall lead to devastation,*  
 *Intransigence shall lead to impasse,*  
 *Peace shall lead to glory.*

## Article I 

The Security Enhancement Coalition adheres to protect the Imperial Federation from anyone who raids on their land. 

## Article II 

The Security Enhancement Coalition obliges to inform the Imperial Federation of any military infrastructure commenced to be built in the immediate vicinity of the Imperial Federation borders within the shortest term the situation allows. 

## Article III 

The Security Enhancement Coalition adheres to never claim any land henceforth declaimed by the Imperial Federation unless the land was claimed by a party not related to the Security Enhancement Coalition or the Imperial Federation gave their agreement for the land to be claimed

## Article IV 

The Imperial Federation shall secede the State of Khione within the borders denoted by The Treaty of Khione. Second Republic of Icarus shall gain full ownership and administration over the lands of The State of Khione upon the secession. 

## Article V 

The Imperial Federation Arsenio Pact treaty shall be the guarantee of peaceful relationships between the Security Enhancement Coalition and the Imperial Federation. 

## Article VI 

The Arsenio Pact shall oversee the Security Enhancement Coalition and the Imperial Federation adhere to obligations stated herein as a representative of the Security Enhancement Coalition treaty and the Imperial Federation Arsenio Pact treaty. 

## Appendix

Immediate vicinity shall be defined by everything within two hundred blocks from the Imperial Federation border and Hyperborean island

Military infrastructure shall be defined as vaults, bunkers, ice roads, bastions, military trenches, snitches, excluding snitches overlapping the Security Enhancement Coalition member states
